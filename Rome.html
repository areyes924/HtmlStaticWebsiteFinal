Roma

Rome is a very nice city. It's near Florence which is also a very nice city.

The Roman Forum, also known as Forum Romanum in Latin, is a rectangular forum surrounded by the ruins of several important ancient government buildings at the center of the city of Rome. It was a site located at the center of the ancient city of Rome and the location of important religious, political, and social activities

The Colosseum, often referred to as the Flavian Amphitheatre in historical texts, is a colossal amphitheater situated at the heart of Rome. Erected during the first century AD, this monumental arena played host to legendary gladiatorial combats and dramatic spectacles. Its immense size, architectural magnificence, and historical significance make it an absolute essential for Rome-bound travelers.
