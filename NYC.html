NEW YORK CITY IS A VERY NICE CITY. YOU SHOULD KNOW SINCE YOU LIVE HERE

The Empire State Building, an iconic symbol of New York City, stands as a testament to human architectural achievement. Soaring 1,454 feet into the Manhattan skyline, it reigned as the world's tallest skyscraper for nearly 40 years after its completion in 1931. Beyond its impressive height, the building's art deco design and illuminated crown make it a dazzling spectacle by day and night.

The Christmas tree outside Rockefeller Center is a beloved holiday tradition in the heart of New York City. Each year, a massive and beautifully adorned tree takes center stage, radiating festive spirit and bringing joy to visitors and locals alike.
