Cairo

Cairo is a very nice city. It's near to Alexandria which is also a very nice city.

The Sphinx in Giza, a timeless marvel, stands guard near the Pyramids of Egypt. This majestic monument, with the body of a lion and the face of a pharaoh, exudes an aura of mystery and grandeur.

Population of Cairo Approximately 22.1 million people in the Metro area as of 2023
Racial Demographics of Cairo Egypt's population is primarily composed of Arabs, with a smaller percentage of Nubians, Berbers, and other ethnic groups.
Geography of Cairo Cairo is located in northern Egypt, along the banks of the Nile River, which flows through the city. It features a mix of urban areas, historic sites, and green spaces.
Geology/Environmental History of Cairo Cairo's geology is characterized by the Nile Delta, which consists of fertile alluvial soil. The city faces environmental challenges, including pollution and water scarcity.
Ruler of Cairo President of Egypt is Abdel Fattah el-Sisi.
